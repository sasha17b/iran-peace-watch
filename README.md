## Readiness for first LP talks — conditions & comfort score

> **Comfort score: 🟠 ~30 / 100 — not ready (early preparation).** Process has moved — a roadmap and working groups now exist — but the market preconditions that make a Partners Group / LGT CP conversation credible remain unmet. Threshold to open first *informal* talks: **~75 / 100**; to open a formal raise: ~90.

What must hold true before a credible first conversation. Progress scale: ○ 0 · ◔ 25 · ◑ 50 · ◕ 75 · ● 100

| Condition | Why PG / LGT CP need it | Progress |
|---|---|:--:|
| Ceasefire durable, no resumption of war | No allocator underwrites an active conflict | ◑ 50 |
| Final framework deal signed and holding | Confirms a real political path exists | ◕ 75 |
| Credible, scheduled sanctions-relief pathway | The legal gate to deploying capital | ◔ 25 |
| Signalled legal irreversibility (UNSC track) | Protection against snap-back risk | ○ 0 |
| Emerging capital convertibility / repatriation | LPs must be able to get money out | ◔ 25 |

**Score bands:** 0–25 not ready · 26–50 early preparation · 51–75 approaching · 76–90 ready for first talks · 91–100 ready to raise.

**Binding constraint:** of the two deal-breakers, the sanctions-relief pathway has edged off zero — a working group exists and Iran claims immediate waivers — but the relief is unverified and unscheduled, and signalled legal irreversibility (the UNSC track) is still at zero. The score can drift up through early preparation but cannot approach the talks threshold until the relief is confirmed and scheduled **and** the irreversibility track turns.
