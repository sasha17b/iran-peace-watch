## Not addressed by the memorandum

Outside the text, and therefore the principal residual risks:

- **Ballistic missiles** — no limits.
- **Regional non-state allies** (Hezbollah, the Houthis, others) — no restrictions.
- **Detainees and prisoners** — no provision.
- **Enforcement** — the monitoring body (#12) observes but carries no stated penalty.

---

## Watch list — leading indicators of genuine progress

1. **A halt to the Lebanon strikes** — Tehran's stated grievance and its off-ramp; the pivot variable.
2. **Sustained Hormuz transit** — physical tanker flow and the Brent price, not the rhetoric, are the real signal.
3. **A signed step out of Bürgenstock** — converts talks into measurable progress.
4. **Published Treasury oil waivers** — the first tangible incentive actually delivered to Tehran.
5. **A constituted monitoring body** — evidence the parties intend enforcement.
6. **A confirmed Geneva date** — the clearest tell that a settlement is within reach.
